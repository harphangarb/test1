💻 Replit Dev Tip for Smooth UX
To make it highly user-friendly on mobile phones inside a hangar, use a simple currentStep state variable ranging from 1 to 20. Clicking the "Next Plane" button simply runs currentStep++ and loads the next array item instantly without reloading the browser window!
